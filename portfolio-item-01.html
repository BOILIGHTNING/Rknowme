Participated in Intel AI For Youth Program

Surveyor AI Humanoid Robot

Project Objective: To use AI to empower humanoid robot for tight space surveillance for construction, rescue & maintenance purposes.

Background: The robot I used, Yanshee, was built by UBTECH and runs on a Raspberry Pi. Considering the dangers to humans in scenarios like rescue operations or sewage cleanup, I explored the idea of using merging robots and artificial intelligence to strategize and explore hazardous locations.

In The End: Successfully presented it to the Vice-president of Intel and the CEO of ITE. Featured in the newspaper in relation to the aim of the Intel AI for Youth program for the young.
