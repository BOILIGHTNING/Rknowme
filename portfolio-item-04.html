Forex Market Prediction AI

Using Machine Learning to Assess Market Trends

Project Overview: I have developed an AI system for predicting trends in the Forex market, utilizing various technical features and strategies such as EMA (Exponential Moving Averages) with intervals of 25, 50, and 100, RSI (Relative Strength Index), Stochastic %K, and others.

Machine Learning Accuracy: The machine learning model achieves a commendable accuracy of approximately 66%, trained on thousands of datasets acquired from the historical view of a specific currency.

Risk Assessment: The primary objective is to assist in risk management by providing a definitive view of potential market movements.

Future Development: While the current model does not incorporate fundamental factors like financial news, I am actively working on integrating these aspects to enhance predictive capabilities.
