About Me

My Journey

Welcome to my digital portfolio! I'm Riydho, a tech enthusiast who has witnessed the transformative evolution of today's technology since childhood. From the advent of smartphones to the rise of artificial intelligence, I've been captivated by the ingenuity behind these advancements and the individuals who turn seemingly impossible concepts into reality. During my time in higher education, I was introduced to the world of programming, a revelation that both surprised and delighted me. Eager to delve deeper into this field, I embarked on a self-directed learning journey, mastering languages such as C, Python, and technologies like Docker, HTML, and CSS. Python, being my language of choice, empowered me to create my own virtual assistant app and participate in the Intel AI for Youth program, where I applied artificial intelligence to address real-world challenges. Driven by curiosity and a passion for innovation, I continuously seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills. Join me on this journey as I showcase my projects, skills, and experiences in the dynamic realm of technology.
