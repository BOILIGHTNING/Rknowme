My Very First Python Project

MY VIRTUAL PERSONAL ASSISTANT

How It Began: I felt confident in myself after a few weeks of learning Python, and I wanted to try something more challenging. I've always wanted to design my own app to improve myself and create something beneficial in my everyday life. So I decided to make my own Virtual Assistant for the project. I spent months researching all of the libraries, dependencies, and structure that I should use to construct my project. After all of my research and understanding, I was finally ready to put together my application.

The Production: For my application, I had chosen the name Luna. Because my program is a virtual assistant, similar to Google's Siri, I had to give it a name! Luna can do a variety of tasks, from opening or browsing Google to sending a tweet to its own followers on Twitter (Luna has its own Twitter account). It has its own voice; for example, when you launch the app, it will greet you with "Good morning, Good afternoon, Good evening" based on the time. Luna can also respond to your voice, allowing you to give it a command, which could make things easier if you don't want to type.

Problems & What can be Fixed in the Future: I had various issues, ranging from missing photographs to the application stopping, but I was able to overcome them all. Though my application is a virtual assistant, it is not as sophisticated as Google or Siri, but it is comparable, and I am pleased that it turned out well for my first attempt. I sincerely hope that in the future, I will be able to acquire newer knowledge that will aid in the improvement and intelligence of my application.

Things that Luna is able to do via Commands (Text and Speech):

  • 1) It can launch the Chrome browser or perform a Google search

  • 2) It has the ability to open a YouTube website

  • 3) Within the app, open the camera and capture a picture)

  • 4) Get an article on whatever you desire from Wikipedia

  • 5) Send an e-mail to anyone using Luna Mail's own service

  • 6) Within the app, send a message via Twitter

  • 7) Retrieve the weather forecast information for any city

  • 8) It displays the current time and date

  • 9) Solve math or geography problems
